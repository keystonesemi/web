KeyStone Enables Telefunken PURE II iDTV Series with Live DAB Audio & Color Slide Show

 12.06.2012
 

KeyStone Semiconductor Corp. (KeyStone), a fabless semiconductor developer of advanced digital radio technologies announces that leading German TV maker, Telefunken, has mass-produced its new PURE II LED iDTV series embedded with KeyStone’s single-chip FM/DAB/DAB+/DMB-A receiver ICs. The new PURE II iDTV series offers 2376 x 1250p high resolution with TV display size ranging from 16” to 32”.

Based on the most advanced integrated digital radio technologies, KeyStone’s Tsunami KSW8650 IC series are world’s most popular low-cost single-chip FM/DAB/DAB+/DMB-A receiver ICs that enable display devices to receive digital audio broadcast as well as color slide show reception. Achieving the lowest power-consumption, smallest form-factor, and the best implementation cost, KSW8650/KSW8650V/KSW8650VQ have received the widest design-wins such as Android and Apple’s iPod/iPhone/iPad accessories, Bluetooth digital radio headsets, Bluetooth digital radio adaptors, FM/DAB cellular phones, FM/DAB digital photo frames, in-vehicle entertainment systems, in-car DAB-to-FM adaptors, in-car DAB-to-FM transmitters, portable DAB/HDMI dongles, automotive-grade DAB adaptors, GPS FM/DAB TMC/TPEG cables, FM/DAB set-top-boxes, DAB/DTV, etc.

Upgradable with KeyStone’s patent-awarded Bluetooth Radio Wizard technology, the above devices can be wirelessly connected to send and to receive data such as slide show, EPG, station information, radio text, etc. between smart phones and the receiving Bluetooth DAB terminals.

Fully certified and compliant with the industry temperature range, KSW8650 families exceed industry-grade temperature-range requirements of -40C to 85C and are the only low-cost FM/DAB/DAB+/DMB-A single-chips satisfying stringent automotive-grade AEC-Q100 requirements. KSW8650 families are best suitable for outdoor or hostile environments where extreme high-temperature and high reception performance are mandated. Samples and demo units are available for ordering. For more information on KeyStone’s digital radio single-chip modules, please contact KeyStone at contact@keystonesemi.com.

About KeyStone
KeyStone Semiconductor Corp. is a technical innovator and leader in wireless digital radio semiconductor. Company is dedicated to providing a series of low-power and low-cost digital radio IC family for today’s multimedia broadcasting market demands. KeyStone offers turn-key solutions from novel Apple and Android apps such as DAB GO!, FM GO!, POWER GO!, KEYSTONE to complete digital radio design platforms to reduce customers’ time-to-market efforts.
KeyStone products enable the delivery of the enriched analog and digital multimedia contents to home and mobile environments. Company provides the industry with the lowest-cost system-on-a-chip turn-key solutions to manufacturers of analog and digital broadcasting access products and portable devices.
